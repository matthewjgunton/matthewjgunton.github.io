Washington DC Metro Guide

How to Use the Machine:

  1. Pick 'B' to get the single use card, unless you're in a group in which case pick 'C' for a group card.
  2. Select how many credits you want to add on. Note you will have a fee of $2 for each new card you get.
  3. Take the card.
  4. Swipe the card as you go through the turnstiles.

How much should I put on the card?

Most people should put $20 -- that should cover most places. If you're doing something specific, here are some recommendations:

    On the weekends, all stops you'd expect to pay $2.50 / person

  • Dulles <> L'enfant -- $6.75 / person
  • Ronald Reagan <> L'enfant -- $2.40 person

On the weekends, expect to pay $2.50 / person

Remember, you can recharge your card at any station!
